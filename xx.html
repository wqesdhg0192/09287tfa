PRIVACY POLICY FOR Subbdtoken.com

Introduction

Welcome to Subbdtoken.com ("we", "our", "us"). We are committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and share your personal information when you visit our website www.Subbdtoken.com (the "Website").

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide to Us:

  • Customer Support Information: When you contact us for assistance, we may collect details such as your full name, email address, and any information you provide to help us resolve your inquiry. This information is used solely for customer support purposes.
  • Opt-In Communications: If you sign up for updates, newsletters, or other communications, we will collect the necessary contact details, including your name and email, for marketing and updates.
  • Accuracy of Information: You are responsible for ensuring that the personal information you provide to us is accurate and up to date.

Information We Automatically Collect:

  • Identifiers: We may collect your IP address, device information, browser type, operating system, and other data about how you interact with our website.
  • Cookies: Our website uses cookies and other tracking technologies (e.g. pixel tags, web beacons) to enhance your experience and functionality. Cookies are small data files stored on your device that help us recognize your browser or device and improve user experience. You can manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect certain functionalities. See our Cookie Policy for more details.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with the website, including your browsing activity, session durations, and engagement with various features.

Information from Third Parties:

  • Third-Party Platforms: If you sign in or interact with our website through a third-party service (e.g., Best Wallet, MetaMask, Google), we may receive information from those platforms, such as user IDs or token balances.
  • Blockchain Transactions: Any blockchain transactions, including token purchases and transfers, are recorded on public ledgers. We do not collect private wallet information but may monitor transaction data through blockchain explorers.
  • Analytics Services: We utilize third-party analytics providers, such as Google Analytics, to help us understand usage patterns and optimize our website. This data includes non-personally identifiable information related to user activity.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the personal information collected for the following purposes:

  • To Provide Services: To facilitate and deliver the services you request, including processing transactions and managing customer inquiries.
  • To Improve Our Website: Understanding how users interact with our website helps us improve functionality, user experience, and content.
  • To Communicate with You: We may send you updates, newsletters, or other information related to SUBBD, based on your subscription preferences.
  • For Legal and Compliance: To comply with applicable legal, regulatory, and security obligations.

3. Sharing of Your Information

We may share your personal information under these circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with third-party service providers who assist with our operations, such as data analysis, email distribution, and customer support.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose personal information if required by law or to comply with valid legal requests from authorities.

4. Security of Your Information

We employ various security measures, including encryption, to protect your personal information. However, no security system is entirely foolproof. We strive to protect your data, but we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Your Data Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights in relation to your personal information. These include:

  • Right to Access: You may request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction: You may request corrections to any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request the deletion of your personal information, subject to certain legal obligations.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: You can request that we limit the processing of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: You may request to receive a copy of your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: If you have provided consent for specific processing, you can withdraw it at any time.

We may request additional information to verify your identity before fulfilling any of these requests. We aim to respond to all legitimate requests within one month, but this may vary based on the complexity of your request.
